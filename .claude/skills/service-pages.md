# Service Pages Management Skill

## Description
Manage the three main service pages for MicroClean: Tapicería y Textiles, Limpieza de Espacios, and Limpieza de Eventos.

## Service Pages
1. **`/servicios/tapiceria.html`** - Upholstery & textile cleaning
2. **`/servicios/espacios.html`** - Home & space cleaning
3. **`/servicios/eventos.html`** - Event cleaning services

## Page Structure
Each service page includes:
- Service hero section with gradient background
- Service details and features
- Before/after gallery
- Contact CTA with WhatsApp integration
- Consistent header/footer navigation

## Key CSS Classes
- `.service-hero` - Hero section with gradient
- `.service-details` - Content sections
- `.before-after-container` - Gallery sliders
- `.contact-cta` - Bottom CTA section

## MicroClean Services

### 1. Tapicería y Textiles
**What we clean:**
- Sofás (Sofas)
- Colchones (Mattresses)
- Sillas (Chairs)
- Alfombras (Carpets)
- Tapicería en cuero (Leather upholstery)

**Method:** Injection-extraction machinery with professional products

### 2. Limpieza de Espacios
**What we clean:**
- Hogares residenciales (Residential homes)
- Apartamentos Airbnb (Airbnb apartments)
- Espacios corporativos (Corporate offices)

**Coverage:** Ciudad de Panamá

### 3. Limpieza de Eventos
**Event types:**
- Bodas (Weddings)
- Fiestas corporativas (Corporate parties)
- Conferencias (Conferences)

**Service:** Before, during, and after event cleaning
